Rural Development

Importance of SHG Formation in Rural Development

Self Help Groups (SHGs) are the cornerstone of rural financial inclusion. By pooling resources and expertise, villagers, especially women, gain the ability to start small businesses and escape the cycle of high-interest local debt. KGVS has been instrumental in forming over 1,000 SHGs in Jhunjhunu, providing training in bookkeeping, group dynamics, and micro-entrepreneurship. These groups not only provide financial support but also serve as a platform for social change, addressing issues like education, health, and sanitation within their communities. The collective power of SHGs fosters a sense of solidarity and empowers individuals to take charge of their own development.
Agri-Innovation

Sustainable Agriculture in Rajasthan

Rajasthan's arid climate presents unique challenges for farmers. KGVS promotes sustainable techniques like drip irrigation, organic farming, and traditional rainwater harvesting (Kunds) to ensure long-term soil health and water security. Our demo farms showcase how minimal water can yield high-quality produce. We also provide guidance on crop diversification, encouraging farmers to grow drought-resistant and high-value crops. By integrating traditional knowledge with modern scientific practices, we help farmers build resilience against climate change and improve their livelihoods while preserving the natural environment for future generations.
Partnerships

Role of NABARD in Rural Growth

NABARD has been a vital partner for KGVS in implementing large-scale rural projects. From Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) to climate-proofing initiatives, NABARD's support provides the necessary resources to scale our impact and bring technological advancements to the remotest villages of Jhunjhunu. This partnership involves not only financial assistance but also technical guidance and monitoring, ensuring that every project achieves its intended goals. Together, we are creating a robust ecosystem that supports rural entrepreneurs, strengthens agricultural value chains, and enhances the overall socio-economic landscape of the region.
Equality

Women Empowerment through SHGs

Empowering women is central to KGVS's mission. Through SHGs, women are gaining financial independence, leadership roles, and a stronger voice in community decisions. Many are now running successful tailoring, dairy, and handicraft units, transforming from housewives to entrepreneurs. Our programs provide them with the skills and confidence to participate in local governance and advocate for their rights. By improving their economic status, we also see a positive impact on their children's education and health. Women in these groups become role models, inspiring other women to pursue their dreams and contribute to the progress of their villages.
Education

Youth Skill Development Opportunities

The future of rural India lies in its youth. IYKRI, under the KGVS umbrella, provides specialized coaching for JET, CUET, and other competitive exams. Beyond academics, we offer vocational training in emerging sectors to ensure our youth are job-ready and capable of driving rural innovation. These programs are designed to meet the evolving needs of the job market and equip young people with the technical and soft skills required for success. By nurturing their talent and providing them with quality education and training, we are helping them build prosperous careers and contribute meaningfully to the development of their communities and the nation.
