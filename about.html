Leadership

Dr. Jaipal Singh

M.Sc (Ag.), Gold Medalist, Ph.D, PGDRD, NET

Dr. Jaipal Singh is a visionary leader in the field of sustainable agriculture and rural development. With an extensive academic background and years of on-ground experience, he established Krishi Avm Gramin Vikas Sansthan (KGVS) to bridge the gap between scientific research and rural farming system.

Under his guidance, KGVS has become a leading NABARD supported NGO, impacting thousands of families across Jhunjhunu and beyond. His commitment to youth development led to the inception of IYKRI, focusing on academic excellence for competitive exams like JET and CUET.

Our Vision

"To develop an ecosystem where agricultural communities can achieve and lead a prosperous and self-sustainable life with dignity."

Our Mission

"Our mission is to support farmers, fishers, and indigenous groups by providing new tools and business training. We want to create great ways of working that can be used everywhere to help communities grow and stay strong."

Core Values

Organization Philosophy

"The organization works on the belief that any development effort cannot be successful unless the people for whom the effort is being made actively participate in the process to make the programme a people's programme."

"The organization believes in pooling the strength, expertise and resources of both governmental and non-governmental organization keeping in mind the need of the targeted beneficiaries."
