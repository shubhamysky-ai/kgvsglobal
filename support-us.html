Why Support KGVS?

Krishi Avm Gramin Vikas Sansthan (KGVS) is a registered non-profit voluntary organization working since 2005 in Rajasthan. Our mission is to empower rural communities, especially women, farmers, and youth, through sustainable agriculture, skill development, financial inclusion, and research-based initiatives.

Your support directly contributes to:

  • Formation and strengthening of Self Help Groups (SHGs)
  • Women entrepreneurship programs
  • Sustainable farming initiatives
  • Youth skill development and competitive exam coaching
  • Water conservation and environmental awareness campaigns
  • Financial literacy programs in villages

Every donation, big or small, helps build stronger, self-reliant communities.

How Your Support Makes Impact

Financial Empowerment

Donations help create and strengthen Self Help Groups that provide micro-loans to women and marginalized families.

Sustainable Agriculture

Supports farmer training, organic farming promotion, water conservation, and integrated farming models.

Women Empowerment

Funds are used for skill development, entrepreneurship training, and leadership programs for rural women.

Youth Development

Through IYKRI, we guide rural youth for competitive exams and create opportunities for brighter futures.
